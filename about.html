About

Benefactor is a German luxury automaker appearing in Grand Theft Auto IV and Grand Theft Auto V games. Based on its vehicle designs, it is the in-game counterpart of Mercedes-Benz, although its diamond-shaped logo slightly resembles Volkswagen's stylized "VW" logo. The term "benefactor" refers to a person who aids an individual or group. Additional vehicles made by Benefactor are featured in The Ballad of Gay Tony. With the exception of the Stretch E, most of Benefactor's vehicles have "special" variants, like their real life Mercedes AMG counterparts, however the changes are purely cosmetic.
